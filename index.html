About Me

Very few activities seem to give me the satisfaction that creating and completing a new design does. Whether it be within Adobe Illustrator or programming in CSS, a new challenge or project is what fuels my passion for design. My background is mostly as a self taught designer and programmer up to this last year. Now that I have more formal education within web design, I feel that my abilities have really matured. When in comes to team experiences, I worked alongside other interns for Abbott Laboratory’s Women in STEM program. This program allowed me to work as a leader amongst my peers in a real business situation.
